## SECTION 7: Platform - "Comprehensive cardiovascular coverage"
Tag: SIX MODULES
Subtext: Each module is built as an offering: it identifies therapy gaps, flags missed interventions, and routes actionable worklists to the right teams.

**6 module cards (3x2 grid):**

1. **Heart Failure** - Surfaces untreated and undertreated HF patients across primary and specialty care - before decompensation occurs.
2. **Electrophysiology** - Identifies rhythm patients who meet criteria for anticoagulation, monitoring, or intervention - but haven't received it.
3. **Structural Heart** - Flags echo findings, referral gaps, and delayed interventions across the structural disease spectrum.
4. **Coronary** - Detects missed opportunities in CAD risk stratification, diagnosis, and escalation of care.
5. **Valvular** - Surfaces progressive valve disease earlier - when intervention can still change outcomes.
6. **Peripheral Vascular** - Expands PAD detection, closes medical therapy gaps, identifies revascularization and limb-preservation candidates, and coordinates longitudinal pathways.

## Wix Setup Steps

1. Go to Wix Editor for your site
2. Choose a blank template or strip-based layout
3. Build each section top-to-bottom following this guide
4. For the contact form: Add > Elements > Form > Contact Form
5. Customize form fields per Section 11 above
6. Set form submission notifications: Dashboard > CRM > Contacts
7. Upload team photos and logos as media
8. Set the site font to Inter (available in Wix fonts)
9. Apply brand colors from the color table above
10. Connect domain: Settings > Domains > tailrd-heart.com
